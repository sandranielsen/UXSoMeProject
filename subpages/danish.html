Admission for Danish students

This page contains frequently asked questions in relation to admission and admission requirements for both Danish and international students. Should you not find the answer you were looking for, book an appointment on the left.

When is deadline for applying for a top up programme?

The deadline is July 1 at 12.00 midday for summer start and December 1 at 12.00 midday for winter start. You apply via the application form on our website.

What kind of documents do I have to upload to apply for a top up?

You just upload the diploma from your AP degree along with the application form – that’s it!

What are the admission requirements for international students?

In order to apply for admission for our higher education programs you have to meet the requirements on the given program ...

You need a Danish qualifying exam for instance a high school or vocational education. The specific requirements varies from program to program.
