Failed exams


What happens if I miss a hand in date?

If it is an exam assignment/project you have 3 attempts for each exam. If you miss the first hand in date, you will have used 1 attempt and you will automatically be signed up for the re-exam/hand in date. Or ask the secretary of your programme. If the missed attempt of hand in/exam is due to illness you need to provide the secretary with a medical statement that documents your illness within 3 (working days) of the hand in date. For additional information on the exam rules look at StudyUpdate or in the Curriculum.



What happens if I do not pass my 3rd exam attempt?

According to the rules you cannot continue the programme and you will be signed out of the programme. If there have been special circumstances regarding your failed attempts you can apply for an exemption to a 4 attempt. Look for further information on StudyUpdate regarding exam rules.
